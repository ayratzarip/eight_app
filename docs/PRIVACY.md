# Privacy Policy for Logbook

**Last updated:** [January 31, 2026]

## Introduction
Logbook ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by Logbook.

This Privacy Policy applies to our application named Logbook (the "App").

## Data Collection and Storage

**1. No Data Collection by Developer**
We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on our servers. We do not have a backend server, and we do not use cloud storage.

**2. Local Storage**
All data you enter into the App (including diary entries, social situations, thoughts, and emotions) is stored locally on your device using a secure local database (SQLite). This data remains exclusively in your possession.

**3. No Third-Party Analytics**
We do not use third-party analytics services (such as Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, or Mixpanel) to track your behavior within the App.

**4. No Account Registration**
The App does not require you to create an account or log in. You use the App completely anonymously.
