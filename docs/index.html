Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 31, 2026

Logbook ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is handled by the Logbook mobile application.

1. No Data Collection

We prioritize your privacy and anonymity above all else.

  • No Server Storage: We do not operate a backend server. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data in the cloud.
  • Local Storage Only: All data you enter into the App (diary entries, triggers, thoughts) is stored locally on your device using a secure local database (SQLite).
  • Full Control: Since the data resides solely on your device, deleting the App will permanently delete all data associated with it.

2. No Tracking

We do not use third-party analytics services (such as Google Analytics, Firebase, or Mixpanel) to track your usage or behavior within the App.

3. No Account Required

The App does not require you to create an account, log in, or provide any personal identification to use its features.

4. Data Usage & Export

Data is used exclusively to provide the App's functionality locally. If you use the "Export" or "Copy for AI" features, data leaves the secure environment of the App only at your explicit command. You are responsible for the security of this data once exported.
